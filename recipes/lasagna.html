ez lasagna

Description

Lasagna so yummy that it will knock your grandma out and drop kick your niece and run over your son and kiss your dog in the lips. Lasagna so easy you can make it while you sleep.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 ounce) jear spaghet sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
